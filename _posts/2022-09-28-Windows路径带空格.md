### 解决方法

在带空格的路径上加双引号

```shell
<div class="wildfire_thread">
<script src="https://utteranc.es/client.js"
        repo="hitptep/hitptep.github.io"
        issue-term="pathname"
        theme="github-light"
        crossorigin="anonymous"
        async>
</script>
</div>
```
